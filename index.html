The Iconic Old Iron Bridge

Constructed decades ago, this historic iron structure serves as the primary gateway to the village. It offers a panoramic view of the vibrant houses lining the Brantas riverbanks.

Historically, this bridge was a simple transport link, but today it stands as a testament to the village's transformation.
