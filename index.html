The Iconic Chameleon

Mural bunglon di Kampung Warna-Warni Jodipan melambangkan adaptasi dan perubahan masyarakat. Seperti bunglon, kampung ini bertransformasi menjadi kawasan wisata kreatif. Warna cerah mencerminkan keberagaman, kebersamaan, dan harapan akan nilai sosial, budaya, serta ekonomi.

The chameleon mural in Kampung Warna-Warni Jodipan represents adaptation and transformation. Just like a chameleon, the village has changed into a colorful and creative tourist destination that reflects diversity, togetherness, and hope.
